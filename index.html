About Me

I started my tech journey over 3 years ago and have since interned in software companies, collaborated on real-world web applications, and done remote work for businesses. I'm a curious and inquisitive learner, always honing my skills one project at a time. I pride myself on completing work to a high standard and going the extra mile whenever possible. I enjoy coding from scratch and bringing my ideas to life in the browser.

I believe in the mobile-first approach and writing concise code. My focus is on creating responsive websites and web apps that offer an optimal experience for users. Although my professional experience is in front-end development, I'm also interested in design. This is because combining design and development experience leads to optimal user experiences in modern websites that always require the integration of design, server technology, and user interaction.
