About Me

Since beginning my tech journey over 3yrs ago, I've interned in startups where I built projects, collaborated with other talented people in shipping real-world web applications, and have also personally done remote work for businesses. I'm naturally curious which makes me an inquisitive learner, and I've been perpetually working on honing my skills one project at a time.

I take great pride in not only being able to complete work to the high standard expected of me but also in going an extra mile wherever possible. I like to code things from scratch, and enjoy bringing my ideas to life in the browser.

As a firm believer of the mobile-first approach and a strong upholder of "fewer lines make better code", I build responsive websites and web apps that provide users the best and most appropriate experience suited to their device and browser by adopting frontend development best practices.

Even though my main experience is in front-end development, I also have a keen interest in design. Given that producing a modern website requires the combination of design, server technology, and the layer that users interacts with, I believe having experience in both design and development allows for making the most optimal user experiences.

Mosco Shopping Store

A scalable e-commerce progressive web application built majorly with React and typescript for the frontend using SCSS and styled components for styling. I used Redux to manage the state of the carts & users, and then Firebase as a database to hold the shop items. There was also an integration of stripe payments to make it look more realistic even though this is actually a personal project and not built for a client.
